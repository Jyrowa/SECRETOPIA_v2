SECRETOPIA

The ultimate party game full of secrets and roasts

How to play?

1. Enter secret

Each player anonymously enters a personal secret - embarrassing, absurd or surprising. Nobody knows who wrote which secret!

2. Guess secrets

The secrets are presented one by one and all players have to guess who they belong to. But the truth remains hidden - only the tips are revealed!

3. Show results

The voting results are shown either anonymously or publicly - this is decided randomly and ensures maximum thrill!

4. Roast round begins

A randomly selected secret becomes the target of creative roasts. Now it's time for quick-witted sayings and merciless comments!

5. Enter roasts

Each player writes their own roast for the secret. But be careful: Whether your roast remains anonymous or appears with your name is unpredictable!

6. Rate roasts

All roasts are revealed one by one and rated by players with emojis - laughter, shock, contempt or respect decide which roast hits the most!
